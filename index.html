My Interests in Accessibility Technology

I am passionate about learning creating inclusive and accessible web experiences for all users. My interests include:

  • Developing web applications that are accessible to people with visual-impairments.
  • Learning implementing ARIA principles to enhance accessibility in my projects.
  • Learning about the different technologies that support people with limb impairments.
  • Advocating for accessibility best practices in web development.
